Group Project - Road Traffic Accident Data in United Kingdom

In this group project, we analyzed Road Traffic Accident Data from the United Kingdom using Power BI and data analytics techniques. The project focused on cleaning and preparing a large dataset containing accident records, weather conditions, road environments, vehicle information, and accident locations. Through data cleaning and preprocessing, we ensured that the dataset was accurate, organized, and ready for analysis and visualization.

  • I learned the importance of data cleaning before performing data analysis
  • I explored how to handle missing, duplicate, and inconsistent data records
  • I understood how accident, weather, and road condition data can be organized for analysis
  • I discovered how clean data improves the accuracy of reports and visualizations in Power BI

Through this project, I learned that data cleaning is an essential step in the data analytics process. I also gained a better understanding of how organized and reliable data can help identify accident trends, support road safety studies, and create meaningful insights through Power BI dashboards and reports.
