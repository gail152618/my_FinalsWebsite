Midterm Task 2- Pivot Tables and Charts in Excel

In this project, I worked on analyzing and visualizing data using Microsoft Excel Pivot Tables and Charts. My tasks included:

  • Creating Pivot Tables to summarize large datasets and calculate totals, averages, and counts efficiently
  • Organizing data into meaningful categories and groups for clear analysis
  • Generating dynamic Pivot Charts to visually represent trends, patterns, and comparisons
  • Applying filters and slicers to interactively explore different aspects of the dataset
  • Formatting tables and charts for readability and professional presentation

Through this task, I gained practical experience in summarizing complex datasets, identifying insights, and creating interactive visualizations in Excel, enhancing both analytical and presentation skills.
